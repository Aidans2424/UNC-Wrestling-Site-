How to Sign Up

Signing up is fast and free. We welcome wrestlers of all skill levels — from beginners to experienced competitors.

  1. Fill out the online interest form (link below).
  2. Attend one of our practice sessions listed on the Practice Schedule.
  3. Bring appropriate athletic clothing and water.
  4. Complete the school's athletics waiver (if required).
